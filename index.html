How it works

  • Create

    We'll upload your Orbit with anything you want - discount forms, rich media, interactive games, up to XGB.

  • Install

    Pop the Orbit anywhere - retail stores, cafes, festivals, conferences.

  • Connect

    Orbit's works by sending out a WiFi signal up to 100m, creating your own captive portal network.

  • Engage

    Customers mobile devices can connect to Orbit via WiFi, without the need to use an app or download to access your content.
